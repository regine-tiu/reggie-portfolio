About Me

I’m a queer, trilingual, UI/UX & Graphic Designer. I specialize in creating accessible websites with an empathic approach. I'm particularly interested in making simple, playful, functional, & memorable websites for my clients! I’m happy to work with my clients in either English, French, or Mandarin. Currently, I split my time between the unceded territory of the Kanien’keha:ka Nation (Tiohtia:ke) and the unceded Mi’kmaw territory (K’jipuktuk).
