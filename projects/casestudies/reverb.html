Reverb Brand Identity

Reverb Sound is an up-and-coming Montreal-based company specializing in experimental audio. They are known for creating interactive sound installations that push the boundaries of our auditory experiences.

Inspired by Swiss design and modern street style, this project plays heavily with the balance between minimalism and repetition to communicate Reverb's cutting-edge approach to sound.
